George Wills (1848-1927)

George Wills was born in 1848 in Wells, Somerset to James and Eleanor Wills. His father was a horse dealer. He operated as an art and antiques dealer in Bath, Bristol and London, collected old Wedgwood (and other antiques), retired in 1919 and died in 1927. He married Georgina Elliott, whose brother Philip Elliott and nephew John P. Way were both in the trade. In London he was in partnership with Elias Simmons as Wills & Simmons. He and Georgina had four children who survived into adulthood and both his sons, John and George, worked in the trade.
